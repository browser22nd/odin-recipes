Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a fresh homemade cheese pizza

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. pre-heat oven to 450F and leave a steel plate inside for at least 10 minutes
  2. using a rolling pin, roll out the dough to a round shape
  3. put tomato sauce in the middle of the circular dough and spread it evenly on all sides
  4. add cheese and make sure to cover the tomato sauce evenly
  5. put the pizza on the steel plate in the oven for around 5 minutes
  6. take off and let rest, enjoy!
