Burger

Description

This is the recipe for a burger

Ingredients

  • Minced meat formed into a patty
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup and Mayo
  • Onions, pickles, Tomato (Preferance)
  • Burger Buns
  1. Put patty on a heated pan
  2. Spread sauces on the bottom bun
  3. When almost done put cheese on the patty
  4. Put the patty on the bottom bun
  5. Put the chosen vegetables on top of the patty, followed by chosen vegetables
  6. Enjoy
